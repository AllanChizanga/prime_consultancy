Welcome to Prime Consultancy Fiscal Management System

About Our Fiscal Compliance System

Welcome to Prime Consultancy's comprehensive fiscal management solution designed to help businesses maintain compliance with fiscal regulations and streamline their financial operations.

Receipt Management

Generate, manage, and track fiscal receipts with full compliance to regulatory requirements. Our system ensures proper receipt formatting, numbering, and digital signatures.

Fiscal Day Management

Open and close fiscal days according to regulatory requirements. Track daily transactions and maintain proper fiscal period records for compliance auditing.

ZIMRA Integration

Seamless integration with ZIMRA (Zimbabwe Revenue Authority) systems for automatic submission of fiscal data, ensuring real-time compliance and reporting.

Customer Management

Maintain comprehensive customer records with proper identification and contact information for accurate transaction tracking and customer service.

Getting Started

To begin using the system:

  1. Ensure your device is properly registered with the authorities
  2. Configure your fiscal device settings in the system
  3. Open a fiscal day to begin processing transactions
  4. Start generating compliant receipts for your customers
